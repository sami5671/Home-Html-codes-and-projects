TOCA

Traditional Italian dishes prepared with locally sourced, seasonal ingredients. The restaurant, a collaboration with renowned chef Oliver Glowig, is also home to Bangladesh's only cheese cave in a hotel.

In collaboration with popular Roman chef Oliver Glowig, Toca restaurant brings classic Italian cuisine to the heart of downown COX'S BAZAR. Using locally sourced seasonal ingredients, we prepare homemade pasta, fresh seafood and traditional dessert, complemented by aged selections from the cheese cave. Toca brings modern twists on classic recipes, and offers take-out or delivery that brings the Ritz-Carlton culinary experience to your home.

Serving: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
Hours: Breakfast daily | 7 a.m. to 11:30 a.m.
Lunch | Monday to Friday 12 p.m. to 2 p.m.
Dinner | Monday to Saturday 5:30 p.m. to 9:30pm

MERMAID BAR


Enjoy hand-crafted classic drinks, signature cocktails and nitrogen martinis at Ritz Bar and the patio.

Relax on the Ritz Bar patio and watch the city come to life while sipping on classic café beverages, locally-crafted beers or our signature cocktails. Enjoy breakfast pastries and sandwiches, and bar food favourites in the evening.

Hours: Monday & Sunday | 7 a.m. to 12 p.m.
Tuesday to Saturday | 7 a.m. to 12 p.m. and 4 p.m. to 11:30 p.m.

EPOCH BAR & KITCHEN TERRACE

At Epoch, we nod to the food & beverage trends of the past and re-imagine them. We are inspired by the gastropub movement. Our concept re-defines pub culture & pays homage to traditional British dining.

Our cocktails respect a notable past full of tradition, but with modern interpretations. We are inspired by the storytelling around us – from the vibrant film industry to the ultimate storyteller, Mother Nature. We tell these tales through creative mixology.

Hours: Sunday to Thursday | 12 p.m. to 12 a.m.
Friday & Saturday | 12 p.m. to 1 a.m.
