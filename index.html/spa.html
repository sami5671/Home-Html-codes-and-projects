Spa myBlend was built upon the expertise of Dr. Olivier Courtin, who believed skin tells one’s emotional and physical story. Based in Paris, these advanced technology myBlend Facial and Body Treatment experiences are now available exclusively in the Americas at this Toronto spa. Personalized skincare is the foundation for a treatment menu that also includes results-driven wellness treatments and massage, borrowing techniques from around the world. The Champagne Nail Bar brings a touch of glamour to classic services, and couples can spend a relaxing day in one of two Cloud 9 suites. Also featured in the 23,000 sq. ft. spa are 16 treatment rooms, an aroma-infused vitality pool, a co-ed sanctuary and relaxation lounges for ladies and gentlemen.
